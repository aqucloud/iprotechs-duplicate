Product Development

We are a highly experienced aquaculture management solutions company with deep domain knowledge and expertise in developing high-quality products. Our team of developers and engineers work tirelessly to create innovative solutions that optimize farm and hatchery operations. Our cloud-based SaaS products, BluH and BluBay, offer advanced features and real-time data to enhance productivity and profitability. Our commitment to quality is reflected in our client-centric approach, and we take pride in providing tailored solutions that meet the specific needs of our clients. With our expertise and commitment to excellence, we are the trusted choice for aquaculture management solutions.

1. BluH:

BluH is a cloud based SaaS solution designed specifically for hatchery operations, providing advanced features to efficiently monitor and control key aspects of shrimp hatchery, such as stocking, maturation, rearing, production costs, and sales. With real-time data and alerts, hatcheries can stay informed about reproductive efficiency and make data-driven decisions to enhance productivity and profitability."

2. BluBay:

BluBay is a comprehensive SaaS-based platform that provides user-friendly interface and advanced analytics to monitor and manage key aspects of farming such as cost of production, feed management, water quality, treatments, and sampling. As a cloud-based platform, BluBay offers real-time data and alerts to help farmers stay informed and make decisions that can improve productivity and profitability.

Together, BluH and Blubay provide a complete, seamless, and Traceable solution for hatchery and Farm management. With these two products, Hatcheries and Farms can easily manage and track all operational aspects, providing an enabling environment to make informed decisions and improve productivity and production.
